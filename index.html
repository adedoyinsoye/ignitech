ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE?

IGNITECH is a non-profit organization focused on training and mentorship of OUT-OF-SCHOOL children form UNDERSERVED Communities to create opportunities and bridge the DIGITAL GAP.

VISION

To create a brand that will make significant impact in communities as regards to empowering the out-of-school children with technology.

MISSION

To create enriched learning communities where the appropriate use of technology creates opportunities for the underserved child.
